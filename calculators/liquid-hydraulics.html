Methodology & Assumptions

Methodology

  • Darcy-Weisbach: ΔPf = f · (L/D) · (ρν²/2) — incompressible liquid friction
  • Turbulent (Re ≥ 4000): Swamee-Jain explicit approximation of Colebrook-White — f = 0.25 / [log(ε/3.7D + 5.74/Re0.9)]²
  • Laminar (Re < 2300): Hagen-Poiseuille — f = 64/Re
  • Transition (2300 ≤ Re < 4000): linear interpolation between laminar and turbulent
  • Static head: ΔPs = ρ · g · Δz / 105 [bar] — sign convention: +Δz = uphill
  • Total: ΔPtotal = ΔPf + ΔPs | Htotal = Hf + Δz

Assumptions

  • Steady-state, single-phase incompressible liquid flow
  • Isothermal — density and viscosity constant along pipe
  • Newtonian fluid — viscosity independent of shear rate
  • Major friction losses only — minor losses (fittings, valves, bends) NOT included
  • Pipe roughness uniform and constant along length
  • Default roughness 0.0457 mm = commercial carbon steel (Moody / GPSA Table 17-7)

Velocity & ΔP Limits (API RP 14E screening)

  • Limits are FEED / preliminary design guidance — not universal hard code-compliance values
  • Vendor confirmation and detailed engineering required before detailed design
