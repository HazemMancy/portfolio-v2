Gas Volume Conversion

Real-gas law conversion between Nm³/h, Sm³/h, Am³/h, Am³/s, SCFM, MMSCFD and ACFM using PV = ZnRT. Z-factor via manual entry, generalized Z, or built-in PR / SRK vapor-root EoS from mixture composition. Same gas composition is assumed between source and target.

ISO 13443 — Natural gas ref. conditions GPA 2172 — MMSCFD basis (60°F, 14.696 psia) Peng-Robinson (1976) & SRK (Soave 1972) Real gas: PV = ZnRT
