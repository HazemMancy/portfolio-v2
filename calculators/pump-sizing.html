Preliminary Centrifugal Pump Details

Methodology

  • Suction head: P_suc = P_vessel + ρg(L_level − CL)/1000 − ΔP_line [kPaa]
  • NPSHa calculated: NPSHa = (P_suc,abs − P_vap)/(ρg). Vendor NPSHr is not calculated.
  • Discharge head: P_dis = P_vessel + ρg(L_level − CL)/1000 + ΔP_line + ΔP_CV + ΔP_eq [kPaa]
  • Differential: ΔP = P_dis − P_suc; ΔH = ΔP × 1000 / (ρg)
  • Hydraulic power: P_hyd = ΔP [kPa] × Q [m³/s] [kW]
  • Pump efficiency: user-entered efficiency if provided; otherwise a screening estimate is used for power only.
  • Reciprocating efficiency: 90% (fixed); Screw: 85% (fixed)
  • Centrifugal shut-off pressure: P_so = P_suc,max + selected shut-off/rated head ratio × ΔP_design. Vendor curve required for final value.
  • Specific speed: N_s = N × √Q [m³/s] / H_stage³⁄&sup4; [SI]
  • Impeller diameter: D = (60/πN) × √(2g × H_stage / ψ) where ψ = 0.75
  • Reciprocating acceleration head: Ha = L × v × N / (C × k × g)

Pump Type Classification (Specific Speed, SI)

  • N_s < 15: Radial Flow (high head, low flow)
  • 15 ≤ N_s < 70: Mixed Flow
  • N_s ≥ 70: Axial Flow (low head, high flow)

Assumptions

  • Single-phase liquid flow; isothermal; ρ and μ constant
  • All pressures are absolute (kPaa / psia) except stated differentials
  • Centrifugal efficiency is estimated from flow only — validate against vendor curves
  • Default shut-off ratio 1.25 is a screening assumption for centrifugal pumps only; verify against vendor curve, especially for multi-stage pumps.
  • NPSH margin allowance is a screening input; final NPSH margin shall follow project/company criteria and vendor NPSHr curve.
  • Screening-level result — subject to project / company criteria
